What is this?

An adapter that turns any keyboard into a Switch controller. Allowing you to play Smash with a keyboard!

Although it was made with Smash Ultimate in mind, this adapter will also be compatible with games like Tetris 99, Super Mario Maker 2, and more!

Features

  • Compatible with any* Switch game
  • Customizable button presets
  • Compatible with most keyboards
  • Recognized as a controller on PC
  • Small form factor
*Excludes motion control games.
